Our Restaurant

Located in the heart of Bunbury, Josephine's Restaurant is a local family owned and operated restaurant with a longstanding reputation for serving delicious homestyle cooking. Bunbury's first Italian restaurateurs, Charles and Rosalba Daqui originally opened Pasta e Vino restaurant in Bunbury in 1982 and 14 years later opened Josephine's Restaurant which they now run with their son, Luke and an experienced team of staff.

Specialising in classic Italian cuisine and fresh seafood dishes, restaurant owner Charles Daqui and Chef Ray Dabic and the team pride themselves on the quality of food and service that brings customers back time and time again.

With a comfortable and relaxed atmosphere, Josephine's is the perfect choice for couples and groups and welcomes families with its kid-friendly menu. Perfected over 29 years in business, the menu includes options for every taste, with the house specialty dish, Toulouse Lautrec, being a favourite for many of our regular customers.

Open for lunch Monday – Friday and Dinner 6 days a week, our friendly staff are always ready to make your dining experience a memorable one.
